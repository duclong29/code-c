The Cottage Garden

The cottage garden is a distinct style of garden that uses an informal design,dense,planting,and a mixture of ornamental and edible plans.

The cottage Garden originated in england and its hisory can be traced back for centuries,although they were re-invented in 1870's England,when stylized were foemed as a reaction to the more structured and rigorously maintained English estale gardens.

The earliest cottage gardens were more practical than their modern descendants, with an emphasis on vegetables and herbs,along with some fruit trees.
