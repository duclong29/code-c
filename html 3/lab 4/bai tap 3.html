The Cottage Garden

The cottge garden is a distinct style of garden that uses an informal design,dense plantings,and a minture of
ornamental and edible plants.

The Cottage Garden originated in Englandand its history can be traced back for centuries, although they were re-inventd
in 1870's England,when styized versions were formed as a reaction to the more structured and rigorously maintained english estate gardens.

The earliest cottage gardebs were more practical than their modem descendants,with an emphasis on vegetables and herbs,along with some fruit trees.
